Como criar, especificar e colecionar embalagens primárias.

Made by Ricardo Junior with Scribe

Follow this guide to update physical product specifications and assemble new educational kits in the system. These steps ensure your products and kits are accurately defined and ready for distribution.



Product Classification

1. Navigate to https://spg.stage.arcocv.co/components/f23c762c-eb7b-40b3-8d68-820f107a3172/edit

2. Select 'Maleta' as the product type.

3. Click 'Salvar Produto' to save classification.

Technical Specifications

4. Navigate to 'Especificação Física' tab.

5. Click 'Adicionar Especificação' to enter technical data.

6. Type technical specifications in the provided field.

7. Enter '0,100' in the 'Peso (kg)' field.

8. Enter internal height measurement.

9. Enter internal depth measurement.

10. Enter external width measurement.

11. Enter external height measurement.

12. Enter external depth measurement.

13. Click 'Salvar' to apply specification changes.

Kit Configuration

14. Navigate to 'Kits' in the sidebar.

15. Click 'Criar' to initiate a new kit.

16. Select 'Kit' for a multi-item collection.

17. Type 'KIT INFANTIL G5 PES' as the kit description.

18. Select 'PES - PES' from the Brand dropdown.

19. Select '27 - Coleção 27' from the Collection dropdown.

20. Select 'INF 5 ANOS' from the Series dropdown.

21. Select 'Vol. 1/L1' from the Volume dropdown.

22. Select 'Aluno' from the User dropdown.

23. Select 'Anual' from the Frequency dropdown.

24. Select 'V1' from the Shipment dropdown.

25. Click 'Criar Kit' to confirm basic information.

Kit Composition

26. Click 'Construir Kit' to add components.

27. Search for and add 'LAB MANUAL MALETA' to the kit composition.

28. Search for and add 'Biology Grade 5 - Lab Manual' to the kit composition.

29. Type handling instructions for the kit.

30. Click 'Confirmar caixa' to validate packaging.

31. Click 'Salvar Alterações' to finalize the kit.
