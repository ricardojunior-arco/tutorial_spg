Como criar um personalizado (e clonar um produto)

Made by Ricardo Junior with Scribe

Learn how to efficiently duplicate an existing product and customize its details for a new SKU. This guide covers the entire process from selecting the original item to finalizing its specifications and status in the system.



Product Initialization

1. Navigate to https://spg.stage.arcocv.co/home

2. Select "Produtos" from the sidebar menu.

3. Click "Criar"

4. Select "A partir de SKU" to clone an existing product.

5. Search for and select the reference product SKU from the results list.

6. Click "Criar e abrir formulário" to proceed.

Basic Information Setup

7. Append " - Ari de Sá" to the product description.

8. Update the "SKU Atual" field with the new SKU identifier.

9. Click "Próximo" to continue to the next setup phase.

Classification and Usage

10. Click "Próximo" again to move to product classification.

11. Enter the required SKU in the "SKU do Material Padrão" field.

12. Select "ARI DE SÁ" from the "Rede" dropdown menu.

13. Click "Salvar Produto"

Physical Specifications

14. Navigate to the "Especificação Física" tab.

15. Click "Editar Especificação"

16. Select step "2" (Detalhes) in the specification wizard.

17. Click "3. Miolo 1" to view the component details.
