Como alterar manualmente o status do funil de um Produto ou Kit?

Made by Ricardo Junior with Scribe

Learn how to efficiently update your product status within the SPG platform. This guide provides a step-by-step walkthrough for searching and transitioning products to the Liberado category.



Product Identification

1. Navigate to https://spg.stage.arcocv.co/home

2. Search for and select product 'Biology Grade 5 - Teacher'.

3. Click the SKU 'TSTBLPROFG527' to copy it to your clipboard.

4. Open the side menu and select 'Gestão do Funil'.

Stage Update

5. Search for the product in the funnel management tool using the SKU.

6. Select 'Liberado', choose a reason for the move, and click 'Mover para Liberado'.

7. Click 'Sim, mover' to confirm the stage change.

Status Verification

8. Return to the home screen and search for the product.

9. Verify that the product status now shows as 'Liberado'.
