Como criar avulsos em massa?

Made by Ricardo Junior with Scribe

Learn how to efficiently generate individual kits for multiple school products in bulk. This guide provides a step-by-step walkthrough of the batch creation process to save you time and manage your inventory effectively.



Bulk Action Navigation

1. Navigate to https://spg.stage.arcocv.co/home

2. Click "Ações"

3. Click "Ações em Massa"

Action Type Selection

4. Select 'Criar Avulsos' to create bulk kits for multiple products.

Product Filter Configuration

5. Open the 'Marca' filter and select 'PES'.

6. Open the 'Série' filter and select '5 ANO'.

Product Selection

7. Click 'Consultar Produtos' to view the filtered list of products.

8. Select the products you wish to include in the bulk kit creation.

Kit Creation Process

9. Click the 'Criar avulsos' button to proceed.

10. Click "Confirmar"

Final Verification

11. Navigate to the 'Kits' section to verify the creation.

12. Select a newly created kit from the list to view its details.

13. Verify that the kit is correctly labeled as 'Avulso'.
