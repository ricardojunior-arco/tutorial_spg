Como alterar o Envio ou Frequência dos produtos em massa?

Made by Ricardo Junior with Scribe

Learn how to efficiently modify frequency and shipping configurations for multiple products simultaneously. This guide streamlines your workflow by showing you how to filter items and apply bulk updates in just a few clicks.



Initial Portal Navigation

1. Navigate to https://spg.stage.arcocv.co/home

2. Select "Importar em Massa" from the Quick Actions dashboard.

3. Navigate to "Ações em Massa" via the sidebar menu.

4. Click "Frequência e Envio" to manage product bulk updates.

Product Filter Selection

5. Select "PES" from the "MARCA" filter dropdown.

6. Select "5 ANO" from the "SÉRIE" filter dropdown.

7. Click "Consultar Produtos" to generate the filtered list.

Bulk Value Configuration

8. Select the checkboxes for the products you wish to edit.

9. Select "Semestral" from the "Frequência" dropdown.

10. Select "V3" from the "Envio" dropdown.

11. Click "Aplicar em 2 produtos" to begin the bulk edit process.

12. Click "Confirmar" to finalize and apply your changes.
