Como revisar para pré-impressão e especificações?

Made by Ricardo Junior with Scribe

Learn how to efficiently update product pagination and confirm specification details within the administrative dashboard. This guide walks you through the step-by-step process of filtering specific items, modifying page counts, and finalizing technical reviews to ensure accuracy.



Product Filtering

1. Navigate to https://spg.stage.arcocv.co/pagination-review

2. Click "Todos"

3. Click "PES"

4. Click "Todos"

5. Click "5 ANO"

6. Click "Carregar"

Pagination Update

7. Set the "Miolo 1" page count to "132".

8. Mark the pagination as reviewed.

9. Click "Salvar (1)"

10. Acknowledge the validation warning regarding page count multiples.

11. Click "Confirmar e salvar"

Specification Review

12. Click "Revisão de Especificações"

13. Select the pending product from the revision list.

14. Click "Ir para conferência" to open the product details.

Component Verification

15. Click "Especificação Física"

16. Verify the pagination status is marked as "Conferida".

17. Click "Componentes"

18. Expand the "CAPA" component section to review details.

19. Expand the "MIOLO" component section to review details.

20. Expand the "ENCARTE" component section to review details.

21. Click "Encadernação"

22. Click "Observação"

23. Click "Especificação Física"

24. Click "Conferir especificação" to finalize the review.

25. Navigate back to the revision list.

26. Open the product from the main database list.
