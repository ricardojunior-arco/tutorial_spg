Como editar um produto no SPG

Made by Ricardo Junior with Scribe

Learn how to efficiently edit product details and adjust grade level categories within the laboratory manual management system. This guide walks you through navigating the interface, updating descriptions, and finalizing your changes to ensure accurate product data.



Product selection

1. Navigate to https://spg.prod.arcocv.co/components

2. Select 'Biology Grade 3 - Lab Manual' from product list

Basic information update

3. Click "Editar Produto"

4. Select 'INF 4 ANOS' in 'Série' field

5. Type 'Biology Grade 3 and 4 - Lab Manual' in 'Descrição' field

6. Click "Próximo"

Classification update

7. Select 'Professor' in 'Usuário' field

8. Click 'Salvar Produto' to save changes
